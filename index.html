Resource Packs

patrix 32x

The Patrix 32x resource pack is a visually appealing addition to Minecraft that enhances the game's textures and visuals with a resolution of 32x. It brings new life and depth to your Minecraft world with crisp and clean textures, vibrant colors, and enhanced details. Compatible with various Minecraft mods, Patrix seamlessly integrates with your gameplay experience. Elevate your Minecraft visuals and immerse yourself in a refreshed and captivating world with the Patrix 32x resource pack.

JICKLUS

The Jicklus resource pack captures the classic Minecraft feel, mixed with a rustic style. It brings together the nostalgic elements of Minecraft while incorporating a touch of rustic charm. With Jicklus, you can experience the familiar blocky world with a unique and appealing aesthetic. For an enhanced visual experience, I highly recommend using Sildur's Shaders alongside the Jicklus pack. Sildur's Shaders will add breathtaking lighting effects, realistic shadows, and other graphical enhancements, truly bringing your Minecraft world to life. To fully enjoy the diverse mob textures included in the pack, I suggest using Optifine. Optifine not only improves performance but also unlocks additional graphical settings, allowing you to customize and optimize your Minecraft experience.

Realistico

Realistico is the new default realism texture pack for Minecraft. Designed with incredible attention to the details, Realistico is changing the way we see Minecraft. Faithful and hyper-realistic textures blended into extremely simple, pixel-perfect surfaces. Realistico brings high quality textures, materic shadows and missing details while keeping the original Minecraft feel in all its beauty.

Faithful PBR 256x

This pack features Ultra detailed PBR textures to give the faithful 32x resourcepack a realistic look when used with shaders. This pack utilises the LabPBR materials like normal mapping, ambient occlusion and subsurface scattering to a realistic and detailed style!
